Maintenance

These simple maintenance steps will help you avoid costly laptop repairs due to hardware failure. As a result your device will last longer and you’ll save money over time on replacement costs.


Keep your laptop well ventilated to prevent overheating because of restricted air flow. Also have it serviced regularly, at least once a year to clean and remove dust and fibres. This will allow air to flow freely so the device runs at its optimum temperature. Use a surge proctor to protect against power surges due to lightning strikes, transformer failure, etc. Impact damage because of a fall is one of the most common causes of hard drive failure, see this Wikipedia page for more information. For laptops use a padded hard shell case to protect against this type of mechanical damage.
